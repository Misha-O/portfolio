About me

I'm Mykhailo,

     My main previous background was solely based around aviation and everything connected to it, as well as customer service, and I had just a basic understanding how web technologies work. However, I made a decision to make it my career, which is also something that needs constant problem-solving skills and where learning never stops.
    I dedicated most of my time to self-learning, as well as finished an online course based on front end technologies such as: HTML, CSS, SASS, jQuery, JS, Node JS.
    A couple of reasons why I want to be a web developer, firstly, I admire the feeling when I visually see that something can be changed or created due to my actions. And secondly, I constantly learn something during my whole life, starting from master’s degree in Ukraine, then whole aircraft structure and equipment as a flight attendant, later on, aviation regulations and diploma, which I received with Honor’s List for academic achievement, what resulted in a flight dispatcher position within Air Canada, next Spanish language which I basically learned by myself and currently have no issues maintaining a full conversation or enjoying a movie.
     Thus, I am certain that I will prove my persistence and dedication to the subject I choose and will be a valuable member of the team. I strongly believe that having such a various background within stress-related industries, constant need to evolve due to life circumstances, genuine love to learn and move the gears of my brain faster, makes me a good team member and a passionate professional.
